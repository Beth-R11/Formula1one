Formula 1

FIA World Championship

Formula 1 is known as the pinnacle of motorsport. The Formula One season consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix which take place in multiple countries and continents around the world on either purpose-built circuits or closed street circuits.


Formula 1 consists of 10 teams made up of 2 drivers per team that compete for two championships: The drivers world championship and the constructors championship. Drivers are awarded points at each Grand Prix from first place down to tenth place.
